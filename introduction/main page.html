ALOO KE PARATHE


Want a lavishing breakfast?

Here's an awesome mouthwatering recipe by my mum so easy that anyone can easily prepare it at thei own. And what's more fab is that you don't have to chop onions or garlic 'cos its free from them, yet tastes heaven!




Maa ke Calcs


    Prep time: 10 mins

    Cooking time: 20 mins

    Total time: 30 mins

    Total servings: 4 parathas






INGREDIENTS:

  • Two boiled and peeled potatoes
  • Two green chillies
  • chopped ginger
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon Asafoetida
  • 1 tablespoon roasted cumin
  • few chopped coriander leaves
  • A pinch of Kasuri Methi
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • salt(as per taste)

RECIPE:

  1. In a pan add the oil and let it heat in high flame for two minutes.
  2. Add the chillies and ginger and saute them for a minute.
  3. Then mash the potatoes and add them along with crushed Asafoetida and Kasuri Methi. Cook it for a while.
  4. Switch the flame off and then add the crushed roasted cumin and sprinkle the coriander leaves.
  5. Make a perfect dough with the flour and roll out small circles. Fill the potato preparation in thr circles and roll them again.For knowing more about hoe to roll chappatis, refer this video.
